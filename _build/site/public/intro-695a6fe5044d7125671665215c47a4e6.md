# Welcome to this very exciting physical chemistry textbook!

This book is designed to give you a reasonable breadth and depth of physical chemistry concepts. It is hoped that by mastering concepts in this text you will be competant to move on to more advance physical chemistry or study instrumentation and biochemical systems with confidence. Most of this text is concise and simple. Most information you will need for this course will be in this book but you may, on occasion, have to look to outside sources for a more full explanation of certain topics.
